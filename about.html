Our Story

Capturing Oklahoma's Heroes

Sooner Response Photography is dedicated to documenting the bravery, professionalism, and daily service of Oklahoma's first responders.

From structure fires and EMS calls to training drills and community events, I focus on telling the real stories behind the uniform — the human side of public safety.

Based in Oklahoma, I travel across the state to capture these powerful moments so agencies, families, and communities can preserve and celebrate them.
